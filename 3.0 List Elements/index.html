Angela's recipe for the best cinnanon rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • cup warm milk
  • teaspoon yeast
  • cup granulated
  • egg plus egg yolk
  • cup butter
  • cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • cup dark brown sugar
  • tablespoon
  • cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix milk
  2. Melt butter
  3. Add in flour
  4. Knead the dough
  5. Transfer the dough
